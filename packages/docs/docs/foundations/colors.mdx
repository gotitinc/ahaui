Color palette listings and general guide to use colors in product design.

## Guideline

### Primary colors

<div className="Grid Grid--smallGutter u-marginBottomExtraSmall">
  {blockColor.primary.map(item => (
    <div key={item.title} className="u-sizeFull md:u-size1of2 xl:u-size2of8 u-marginBottomMedium">
      {renderBlockColor(item, 'u-paddingHorizontalExtraSmall u-paddingBottomTiny u-paddingTopMedium')}
    </div>
  ))}
</div>

Our primary palette is comprised of blue, dark neutral, white, and orange. These colors are present across most touch points from marketing to product.

* Blue Blue300 -  `#375DE7` is used for primary actions and buttons, links, for indicating progress, and representing authentication.
* Dark neutral Neutral800 -  `#172B4D`  is used primarily for body text and headings.
* White White -  `#FFFFFF`  is used for page backgrounds and anything that is white.
* Orange Orange300 -  `#ED6200`  is used like accent color for ask flow and payment flow.

### Secondary colors

<div className="Grid Grid--smallGutter u-marginBottomExtraSmall">
  {blockColor.secondary.map(item => (
    <div key={item.title} className="u-sizeFull md:u-size1of2 xl:u-size2of8 u-marginBottomMedium">
      {renderBlockColor(item, 'u-paddingHorizontalExtraSmall u-paddingBottomTiny u-paddingTopSmall')}
    </div>
  ))}
</div>

### Neutrals

There are various shades of neutral colors. They are used for text and subtle backgrounds when we don't want to make too much attention to less important information or convey information such as `disabled`.

* Red Red300 -  `#D0021B`  is used for negative alerts and messages.
* Yellow Yellow300 -  `#FFAB00` is used for warning alerts and messages.
* Green Green300 -  `#E3B000`  is used for positive notications and messages.


<div className="Grid Grid--smallGutter u-marginBottomExtraSmall">
  {blockColor.neutrals.map(item => (
    <div key={item.key} className="u-sizeFull md:u-size1of2 xl:u-size4of12 xxl:u-size2of8 u-marginBottomMedium">
      <h6 className="u-marginBottomExtraSmall">{item.title}</h6>
      {item.list.map(color => (
        <React.Fragment key={color.title}>
          {renderBlockColor(color, 'u-paddingHorizontalExtraSmall u-paddingVerticalTiny u-marginBottomTiny')}
        </React.Fragment>
      ))}
    </div>
  ))}
</div>

### Extended palette

Extended palette is all the useable tints and shades of each color in the palette. It can be used for background, illustrations and components in product.

<div className="Grid Grid--smallGutter u-marginBottomExtraSmall">
  {blockColor.extended.map(item => (
    <div key={item.key} className="u-sizeFull md:u-size1of2 xl:u-size4of12 xxl:u-size2of8 u-marginBottomMedium">
      <h6 className="u-marginBottomExtraSmall">{item.title}</h6>
      {item.list.map(color => (
        <React.Fragment key={color.title}>
          {renderBlockColor(color, 'u-paddingHorizontalExtraSmall u-paddingVerticalTiny u-marginBottomTiny')}
        </React.Fragment>
      ))}
    </div>
  ))}
</div>
